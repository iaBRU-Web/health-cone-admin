Honest Limitations
What We Can't Do Yet
HealthCone is a growing project. Here are the current limitations we're working to address.
‚åö

No Own Smartwatch

We cannot manufacture our own smartwatches yet. HealthCone works as a web-based platform that integrates with compatible third-party wearables.

üîã

Battery Life

Continuous monitoring shortens wearable battery life significantly. Sensor data accuracy also depends on the quality of the connected device.

üåê

Internet Required

Real-time sync requires an active internet connection. In areas with poor connectivity, alerts may be delayed ‚Äî a critical challenge in rural Rwanda.

ü§ñ

AI Model Upgrades Needed

Our AI models are not yet fully upgraded, which can result in suboptimal response quality. Budget is the primary constraint. Upgrading AI models costs 300,000+ RWF.

üîí

Privacy & Cloud

Health data stored in the cloud raises privacy concerns. We're working toward a compliant, encrypted architecture that meets HIPAA-equivalent standards.

üè¶

High Hospital Onboarding Costs

Integrating hospitals into the network requires technical setup and staff training. High implementation costs slow expansion of the hospital network.
