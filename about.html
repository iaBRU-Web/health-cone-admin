The Problem We Solve
Why HealthCone Exists

In Rwanda and across East Africa, cardiac emergencies are often fatal simply because help arrives too late. Patients don't know they're in danger, emergency contacts aren't notified fast enough, and hospitals have no advance warning.

HealthCone was designed as a direct answer to this. By placing continuous AI-powered monitoring on the wrist ‚Äî through any compatible smartwatch ‚Äî and connecting directly to hospital emergency networks, HealthCone transforms a reactive system into a proactive one.

Traditional wisdom tells us that the body always gives warning signs. Modern science gives us the tools to read those signs in real time. HealthCone brings both together in a platform that is accessible, scalable, and built with Rwandan infrastructure in mind.
